TherapyVR

Overcome your phobias through Virtual Reality and understand the experience of victims that have different phobias in one simulation.

TherapyVR Demo

These simulations are meant to be a form of exposure therapy to phobias in hopes that they allow the user to grow comfortable with the subject. The user will be put in a phobia simulation of thier choosing. The simulations for the specific phobia will vary in intensity as the user progresses through rounds. At the end of each round, the user will answer a small survey that will judge whether they are ready to move on to the next round of therapy.
